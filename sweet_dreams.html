Sweet Dreams

Game Overview

Sweet Dreams is a 3D First-person magic survival game. This game is being made in Unity and will be my Dissertation project which I am developing over my final year of University. The story of the game is a secret for now but the objective is to survive the rounds of zombies using magical gear.

Inspirations

The weapons are inspired by games such as 'Spellbreak' and 'Ziggurat 2'. The style of zombie survival and perks will be like 'Call of Duty: Zombies' but the play style will be similar to 'Borderlands 2' (explosive and crazy)!
